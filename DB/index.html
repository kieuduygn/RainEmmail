Customers now receive a free extra day added to their Eurail One-Country, Eurail 2-Country and Eurail Scandinavia Pass purchased during the promotional period.

Booking Period Between March 1st, 2015 and April 30th, 2015
Travel Period The promotional pass must be validated within 6 months of purchase

Children When traveling with a Eurail pass, children under the age of 4 on the first day of travel, go for free with an adult (18 years or older). Up to 2 children between the ages of 4 and 11 can travel for free with an adult. If you want your child to have their own seat (or bed/couchette on night trains), a separate reservation will need to be made at an additional cost for the child.

Youth If you or anyone in your party is a traveler between 12 - 25 years of age on the first date of travel.

Group Small groups of 2 to 5 people traveling together are eligible for special savings with the Saverpass. All travelers must simply be present at the start to validate their pass (and always travel together for France Saver Passes).
